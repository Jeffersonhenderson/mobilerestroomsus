Frequently Asked Questions

1. What if my event location is remote and has no access to water or power?

That's no problem at all. All of our standard and deluxe portable restrooms in Oak Hill, OH are completely self-contained. They do not require any external water or power connections. Even our luxury restroom trailers can be outfitted with onboard freshwater tanks and quiet generators to operate in fully off-grid locations.

2. How do you determine the final cost for a porta potty?

The final porta potty rental cost in Oak Hill, OH is based on three main factors: the type of unit you choose (a luxury trailer is more than a standard unit), the duration of your rental (a month-long rental has a lower per-day cost than a one-day rental), and the delivery distance to your location. Our quotes are always transparent and all-inclusive.

3. What is your cleaning and sanitization process between rentals?

We have a rigorous, multi-step process. Every unit is completely evacuated, then power-washed inside and out with a hospital-grade disinfectant. We pay special attention to all high-touch surfaces. It's then air-dried, restocked with fresh, sealed supplies, and given a final quality control inspection before it’s certified "ready to rent.

4. Can I choose the color of my portable toilet?

While we cannot guarantee a specific color, we do maintain a fleet in a variety of standard colors (like blue, green, and gray). For events where aesthetics are a key concern, such as weddings, we can often provide units in more neutral colors upon request, subject to availability. Please mention this when you book.

5. How much notice do I need to give to place an order?

For standard units, we can often accommodate requests with 24-48 hours' notice. However, to ensure you get the exact type and number of units you need, especially for specialty units like luxury trailers or for weekend rentals, we highly recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance.
